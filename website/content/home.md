## What is Project Garuda?

Project Garuda is an open exploration of **Constitutional Engineering** — a disciplined approach to establishing legitimacy before action, governance before implementation, and stewardship across generations.

## Why does it exist?

Intelligence deserves legitimacy as well as capability. Project Garuda exists to help close the gap between powerful technologies and the institutions capable of guiding them wisely.

## What is Constitutional Engineering?

A discipline concerned with the deliberate design, governance, preservation, and long-term stewardship of institutions — asking *what makes building legitimate* before asking *what can be built*.

## Why should I care?

The future of intelligent systems will be determined not only by capability, but by the quality of the institutions that guide them.
