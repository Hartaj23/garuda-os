Project Garuda is guided by ten principles — the institution's enduring compass:

1. Responsibility Before Capability
2. Authority Before Action
3. Evidence Before Assumption
4. Governance Before Momentum
5. Clarity Before Complexity
6. Stewardship Before Ownership
7. Preservation Before Expansion
8. Transparency Before Convenience
9. Continuity Across Generations
10. Humility Before Certainty
