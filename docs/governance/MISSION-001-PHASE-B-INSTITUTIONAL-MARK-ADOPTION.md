## Founder Decision

The Founding Generation hereby adopts **C-03 Baseline** as the official institutional mark of Project Garuda.

This decision follows the completion of the approved legitimacy sequence:

- Visual Philosophy v1.0
- Design Language v1.0
- Design Tokens v1.0
- Canonical Page Specification v1.0
- Institutional Mark Exploration v1.0
- Institutional Mark Selection v1.0
- Founder Recommendation for Institutional Mark Adoption

The Founding Generation finds that the evidentiary standard established for adoption has been satisfied.

C-03 Baseline has demonstrated that it acknowledges an institutional identity already established through disciplined design rather than attempting to create one through visual symbolism alone.

Accordingly, the institution adopts C-03 Baseline as its official institutional mark.

---

## Authority

This decision adopts the institutional mark only.

It does not authorize:

- CSS implementation,
- website implementation,
- favicon creation,
- public deployment,
- external communications,
- or any other implementation activity.

Implementation remains subject to separate Founder authorization.

---

## Institutional Principle

Project Garuda affirms that its identity resides in its constitutions, governance, architecture, engineering, preservation, and public philosophy.

The institutional mark exists to acknowledge that identity.

It does not define it.

Should a future generation determine that a different mark better serves the institution, that decision shall be undertaken through the same disciplined governance by which this mark has been adopted.

The institution preserves legitimacy above permanence.

---

## Closing Declaration

With this decision, Mission 001 — Phase B reaches its constitutional conclusion.

The Founding Generation has established not merely an institutional mark, but an institutional method for adopting one.

That method now becomes part of the institution's inheritance.

C-03 Baseline shall accompany Project Garuda with humility, restraint, and quiet confidence, reflecting the discipline through which it was earned.

The institution remains greater than its symbol.

May the symbol always remember the institution it serves.

---

End of Institutional Mark Adoption
