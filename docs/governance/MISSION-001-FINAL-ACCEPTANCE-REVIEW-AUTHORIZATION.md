## Founder Decision

Mission 001 — Final Acceptance Review is hereby authorized.

The purpose of this review is to determine whether Mission 001, in its entirety, faithfully delivered the Public Foundation authorized by the Founding Generation.

This review shall not propose enhancements, redesigns, or future work.

It shall answer one question only:

**"Is this the website the institution intended to build?"**

---

## Scope

The review shall examine Mission 001 as a complete institutional program, including:

- Phase A — Site Architecture
- Phase B — Brand Foundation
- Phase C — Implementation

The review shall verify that:

- implementation faithfully reflects all approved institutional decisions,
- repository-first authority remains intact,
- the public website introduces the institution without replacing the repository,
- the implemented visual system faithfully expresses the approved philosophy, design language, tokens, Canonical Page Specification, and Institutional Mark,
- no unauthorized conceptual decisions entered during implementation.

---

## Explicitly Out of Scope

This review shall not consider:

- future enhancements,
- feature requests,
- deployment strategy,
- marketing,
- analytics,
- social channels,
- search engine optimization,
- additional content.

Those belong to future missions.

---

## Success Criteria

Mission 001 shall be accepted when the Founding Generation concludes that:

- the implemented website faithfully represents the institution,
- engineering introduced no new institutional judgment,
- the website serves as an introduction while the repository remains the canonical authority,
- the completed work satisfies the original authorization.

If these conditions are met, Mission 001 shall be formally closed.

---

## Institutional Guidance

Acceptance is not a declaration of perfection.

It is a declaration of fidelity.

The institution accepts work when it truthfully reflects what was authorized, not when every conceivable improvement has been exhausted.

That discipline preserves institutional momentum without sacrificing institutional integrity.

---

## Closing Statement

Mission 001 began with a question:

*"How should Project Garuda introduce itself to the world?"*

The Final Acceptance Review determines whether that question has been answered faithfully.

If accepted, Mission 001 will stand as the institution's first completed public program.

The remaining decision will concern only visibility.

The institution itself will already be ready.

---

End of Final Acceptance Review Authorization
