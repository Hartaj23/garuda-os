## Purpose

This governance record records the Founder's decision regarding **GAR-SPRINT-0013 — Investment
Intelligence Foundation**.

Its purpose is to determine whether GAR-SPRINT-0013 faithfully translates ADR-0014 into a complete
and implementation-ready engineering specification while preserving constitutional authority.

This approval concerns the sprint specification only.

It does not authorize engineering implementation.

## Sprint Review

The Founder has reviewed GAR-SPRINT-0013 against:

- GAR-0021 — Investment Intelligence Constitution v1.0
- ADR-0014 — Investment Intelligence Architecture v1.0
- GAR-SPRINT-0013 Sprint Audit

The review confirms that the sprint:

- faithfully derives from constitutional authority
- faithfully derives from architectural authority
- preserves Evidence Integrity
- preserves Human Authority
- preserves Constitutional Refusal
- preserves Explainability
- preserves Traceability
- remains implementation-independent where appropriate
- provides a complete engineering roadmap
