## Purpose

This document establishes the constitutional baseline of Project Garuda following the completion
of Governance Cycle 6.

It is **not** constitutional law.

It creates no architectural, sprint, or engineering authority.

Its purpose is to provide a single institutional reference describing the state of Project Garuda
after completion of its first descriptive external-capability constitutional epoch.

The repository remains the sole institutional source of truth.

When this document conflicts with a GAR constitution, ADR, approved sprint specification, or
committed repository state, the higher-authority artifact prevails.

---

## Institutional Mission

Project Garuda is a constitutional operating system for intelligent systems.

Its objective is to ensure that every stage of institutional evolution is governed before it is
engineered.

The project is founded upon the principles of:

- Repository-first governance
- Constitutional Engineering
- Explicit authority
- Immutable institutional history
- Governance before architecture
- Architecture before implementation

Every repository artifact exists within an explicit governance chain.

No engineering authority exists without constitutional authority.

---

## Institutional Evolution

The first descriptive external-capability constitutional epoch established the foundations required
for external capability governance under Descriptive Before Operational law.

This evolution progressed through successive constitutional extensions and architectural
interpretations while preserving strict separation between governance and implementation.

During this epoch the institution completed:

- Platform Core
- Phase I Cognitive Foundations
- Interface Foundation
- Integration Foundation
- Runtime Foundation

Architecture 5 established the institutional governance model separating:

- Institutional governance
- Architectural governance
- Engineering governance

Governance Cycle 6 subsequently evaluated whether additional descriptive external-capability
foundations remained constitutionally admissible.

Following constitutional analysis, GAR-0020 concluded that no further descriptive foundation
satisfied the governing constitutional principles.

The Founder ratified this conclusion, establishing an **epoch pause** as the constitutional
baseline.

## Governance Model

Project Garuda operates through explicit institutional authority.

Authority progresses through clearly separated governance stages:

```
Founder Decision
        ↓
Institutional Recommendation
        ↓
Founder Authorization
        ↓
Governance Artifact Drafting
        ↓
Founder Ratification
        ↓
Architectural Authorization
        ↓
Sprint Authorization
        ↓
Engineering Authorization
```

Authority never transfers implicitly.

Every transition requires an explicit recorded decision.

See [INSTITUTIONAL-STRATEGIC-LIFECYCLE-v1.0.md](INSTITUTIONAL-STRATEGIC-LIFECYCLE-v1.0.md) for the
three-layer institutional model.

## Meaning of Institutional HOLD

Institutional HOLD is an intentional governance state.

It signifies that:

- the current constitutional baseline is complete,
- no additional constitutional work is presently authorized,
- no architectural work is presently authorized,
- no sprint work is presently authorized,
- no engineering work is presently authorized.

Institutional HOLD represents governance maturity rather than inactivity.

---

## Future Constitutional Evolution

Future constitutional evolution is neither presumed nor prohibited.

A new Governance Cycle may begin only through explicit Founder authorization.

The opening of a future cycle does not imply:

- a new constitutional extension,
- a new architectural record,
- a new sprint,
- or engineering work.

Each must be independently justified and authorized under the governing institutional framework.

## Institutional Principles

Project Garuda shall continue to operate according to the following enduring principles:

- Governance precedes architecture.
- Architecture precedes implementation.
- Repository artifacts define institutional truth.
- Explicit authority is required for every governance transition.
- Completed history remains immutable.
- Constitutional expansion occurs only when justified by demonstrable institutional necessity.

## Closing Statement

Project Garuda has completed its first descriptive external-capability constitutional epoch.

The institution now possesses a ratified constitutional corpus, a mature governance framework, and
an auditable authority model that clearly distinguishes decision, recommendation, authorization,
drafting, ratification, architecture, sprint planning, and engineering.

The constitutional baseline established in this document provides the foundation from which any
future institutional evolution, should it become necessary, will begin.

Until such time, Project Garuda stands at **Institutional HOLD** under **Constitutional Baseline
