## Purpose

This governance record records the Founder's decision regarding **ADR-0014 — Investment
Intelligence Architecture**.

Its purpose is to determine whether ADR-0014 faithfully translates the constitutional authority
established by GAR-0021 v1.0 into an implementation-independent architectural framework.

This approval concerns architecture only.

It does not authorize implementation.

---

## Architectural Review

The Founder has reviewed ADR-0014 against the approved Architectural Audit.

The review confirms that ADR-0014:

- derives from GAR-0021 v1.0
- preserves the authority of the Constitutional Evidence Base Edition 1.0
- remains implementation-independent
- remains technology-neutral
- preserves Evidence Integrity
- preserves Human Authority
- preserves Constitutional Refusal
- preserves Justificatory Explainability
- preserves Constitutional Traceability
- preserves Constitutional Distinctions
- preserves Constitutional Tensions

---

## Architectural Finding

The Founder determines that ADR-0014 faithfully translates constitutional authority into
architectural form.

Architecture neither expands nor weakens constitutional authority.

Architecture exists solely to express constitutional law in a form suitable for future
implementation.

---

## Approval

Accordingly:

**ADR-0014 — Investment Intelligence Architecture**

is hereby

**APPROVED**

as the authoritative architectural baseline for the Investment Intelligence domain.

Draft v0.8 is elevated to **Approved v1.0**.

---

## Authority

ADR-0014 shall become the architectural authority for future Investment Intelligence
implementation after certification, freeze, and publication.

Future sprint specifications shall derive from ADR-0014.

Future implementations shall derive from approved sprint specifications.

Authority therefore remains:

```text
GAR Constitutions
        │
        ▼
GAR-0021
        │
        ▼
ADR-0014
        │
        ▼
GAR-SPRINT-0013 (future)
        │
        ▼
Implementation (future)
```
