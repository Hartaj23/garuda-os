## GAR-0020 Drafting Scope

The draft shall include:

1. Re-evaluation of the post-Runtime constitutional landscape
2. Examination of descriptive Orchestration as a candidate constitutional foundation
3. Assessment of alternative constitutional directions
4. Determination whether a new descriptive foundation is constitutionally admissible
5. Consideration of an epoch pause if no admissible foundation exists
6. Preservation of all previously ratified constitutions unless an amendment is explicitly proposed
   and justified

The draft shall conclude with a **constitutional recommendation** and must **not** authorize
architectural work, sprint work, or engineering.
