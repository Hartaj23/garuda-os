## Founder Decision

The Founding Generation hereby authorizes the public deployment of the Project Garuda Public Foundation.

This authorization follows the successful completion and acceptance of Mission 001, including:

- Phase A — Site Architecture
- Phase B — Brand Foundation
- Phase C — Implementation
- Final Acceptance Review

The Founding Generation finds that the Public Foundation faithfully represents the institution established through the First Constitutional Epoch and the Launch Program.

Accordingly, public deployment is authorized.

---

## Scope

This authorization permits:

- deployment of the approved public website,
- publication through the approved hosting environment,
- use of the adopted Institutional Mark v1.0,
- public access to the approved gateway pages,
- operation of repository-first navigation,
- public availability of the institutional reading journey.

---

## Institutional Commitments

Public deployment shall preserve the following principles:

- The website introduces the institution.
- The repository remains the canonical authority.
- Public communication shall faithfully represent approved institutional artifacts.
- Future changes shall continue to follow established governance.

Deployment grants visibility.

It does not grant authority to alter the institution outside its constitutional processes.

---

## Success Criteria

Deployment shall be considered successful when:

- the public website is accessible,
- repository links function correctly,
- the implemented experience faithfully matches the accepted Public Foundation,
- the institution remains represented with clarity, restraint, and integrity.

## Closing Declaration

Mission 001 is complete.

The Public Foundation has been conceived, governed, designed, implemented, reviewed, accepted, and authorized for public deployment through explicit institutional legitimacy.

The Founding Generation therefore concludes the first public program of Project Garuda.

From this day forward, the institution enters its public life.

May it remain worthy of the trust it seeks to earn.

May every future generation steward it with the same discipline through which it first came into the world.

Project Garuda is now authorized to meet the world.
