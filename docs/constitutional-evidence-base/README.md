## Constitutional Position

The Constitutional Evidence Base is **descriptive** rather than normative.

It exists to discover enduring principles through disciplined inquiry.

It establishes no constitutional obligations and authorizes no architectural or engineering work.

Constitutional authority begins only after principles are synthesized, reviewed, and ratified through
**GAR-0021** — a path that is **not** authorized by this corpus.

Evidence is not law.
Law is not architecture.
Architecture is not implementation.

These distinctions are the integrity of Project Garuda.

---

## Purpose

The Constitutional Evidence Base (CEB) is the authoritative research corpus supporting the
constitutional development of Investment Intelligence within Project Garuda.

Unlike architectural documents, implementation specifications, or product documentation, the CEB does
not prescribe how systems shall be built.

Instead, it records, organizes, and synthesizes enduring evidence regarding markets, investing,
trading, decision-making, risk, and human behaviour.

Its purpose is to ensure that every constitutional principle later adopted within Investment
Intelligence can be traced to documented evidence rather than opinion, fashion, or convenience.

Someone reading these pages a decade from now should understand immediately:

- why this corpus exists,
- what authority it does and does not possess,
- how it fits the Garuda constitutional process,
- and why discovery was required before definition.

## Why the CEB Exists

Garuda OS provides a constitutional operating system for intelligent systems.

Book Two asks a different question:

> allocate, or act?

That question cannot be answered by indicators, APIs, or models alone.

It requires evidence — carefully gathered, comparatively examined, and preserved without premature
resolution.

The CEB is that evidence.

No constitutional article shall be introduced without evidence.

## Research Standard

Every observation considered for eventual constitutional elevation shall be evaluated against five
criteria:

| Criterion | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Universality** | Does it hold across markets, geographies, and asset classes where the claim applies? |
| **Timelessness** | Does it endure beyond a particular technology, decade, or fashion? |
| **Evidence** | Is it supported by documented inquiry rather than assertion? |
| **Technology neutrality** | Can it be stated without committing to a platform, vendor, or implementation? |
| **Constitutional necessity** | Would future architecture or implementation be incomplete or unsafe without it? |

Only observations that survive these criteria may become *candidate* constitutional principles.
Candidacy is not ratification. Elevation requires a separately authorized constitutional process.

---

## Research Philosophy

The objective of the CEB is not to discover the most successful investor.

The objective is to discover the most enduring principles.

Consensus, disagreement, historical evidence, academic research, practitioner experience, and
institutional knowledge are all admissible when properly documented.

The Constitutional Evidence Base seeks **convergence** rather than popularity.

It asks, wherever possible:

> What must be true of every market, regardless of time, geography, technology, or asset class?

That is constitutional inquiry — not literature summary.

---

## Evidence Integrity

The Constitutional Evidence Base shall remain descriptive throughout its development.

Researchers may record competing schools of thought, contradictory evidence, unresolved questions,
and minority viewpoints without resolving them.

Resolution occurs only during constitutional synthesis.

Evidence shall not be rewritten to support predetermined constitutional conclusions.

This corpus must remain a faithful record of discovery — including where experts disagree.

## Permanent Governance Doctrine

### Principle of Constitutional Humility

No constitutional principle shall be adopted merely because it appears persuasive.

Every constitutional principle shall survive disciplined inquiry, comparative analysis, and
documented evidence before it may govern future architecture or implementation.

This doctrine applies to all future constitutional epochs unless explicitly superseded by
constitutional authority.

## Scope

The Constitutional Evidence Base studies timeless principles governing investment intelligence,
including:

- Markets
- Human behaviour
- Economics and macroeconomics
- Businesses and value creation
- Market structure
- Trading and probability
- Risk and portfolio intelligence

languages, platform decisions, broker integrations, APIs, trading logic, and product features.

## How to Read This Corpus

Read the CEB as evidence, not as instruction.

Do not treat a well-argued passage as constitutional law.
Do not treat disagreement as failure.
Do not treat synthesis as permission to build.

When a future constitution cites these volumes, the citation must be auditable.
When a future architecture interprets that constitution, the interpretation must remain subordinate.
When engineering finally begins, it must inherit both the law and the evidence that justified it.

That is how Garuda preserves integrity across generations.
