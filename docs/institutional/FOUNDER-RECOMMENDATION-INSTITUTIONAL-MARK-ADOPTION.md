## Recommendation

Following completion of the approved legitimacy sequence, review of the Canonical Page Specification, the Stranger's Report, the print exercise, and Institutional Mark Selection v1.0, the Founder recommends that the Founding Generation authorize **Institutional Mark Adoption**.

This recommendation is made on the basis of institutional evidence rather than preference.

The recommended mark, **C-03 Baseline**, has demonstrated that it does not seek to create the identity of Project Garuda.

It quietly acknowledges an identity that has already been established through the institution's visual philosophy, design language, design tokens, and canonical page structure.

The institution therefore satisfies the principle established at the outset of Phase B:

> *An institution is ready to adopt its mark when removing the mark no longer removes its identity.*

The evidence gathered throughout Mission 001 indicates that this condition has been met.

Accordingly, the Founder recommends that the Founding Generation proceed to the formal consideration of **Institutional Mark Adoption** as a distinct institutional act.

This recommendation does **not** itself adopt the mark.

The institution shall continue to distinguish clearly between:

- exploration,
- selection,
- adoption,
- implementation.

That distinction preserves the discipline through which Project Garuda has been built.

---

## Founder Reflection

Every enduring institution eventually adopts a symbol.

The significance of that symbol lies not in its artistic complexity, but in the integrity of the process through which it was chosen.

Project Garuda has treated its institutional mark with the same discipline that it applied to its constitutions, governance, architecture, engineering, and preservation.

For that reason, the recommended mark is not merely a graphic.

It is the visible acknowledgment of a process conducted with legitimacy, transparency, and deliberate stewardship.

Whether future generations retain this mark or someday replace it through equally disciplined governance is a decision that belongs to them.

Our responsibility was not permanence.

Our responsibility was legitimacy.

That responsibility has been fulfilled.

This recommendation is signed in support of advancing to the final Founder Decision for Institutional Mark Adoption.

May the institution continue to wear its symbol with the same quiet confidence through which it earned it.

## The Adoption Print Exercise

Prescribed before permanence. Conducted as part of the evidence review.

Print the [Canonical Page Specification v1.0](../../website/brand/CANONICAL-PAGE-SPECIFICATION-v1.0.md) twice — without the mark, and with C-03 Baseline at header placement (24px, per specification).

Ask only one question:

> **"Does the page become more itself — or merely more branded?"**

The evidence indicates C-03 makes the page feel **more like itself**.

Adoption has been earned.
