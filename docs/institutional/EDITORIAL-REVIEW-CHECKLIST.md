The Editorial Review Checklist provides a practical framework for reviewing every volume within the Preservation Series.

Its purpose is consistency.

Every editorial review should evaluate the same areas in the same order, ensuring that future editions preserve the institutional standards established during the First Edition Editorial Pass.

Completion of this checklist does not create institutional authority.

It confirms editorial completeness.
