# A Blessing for the Institution

| Field | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Document Type | Institutional Literature |
| Authority | Informational (Non-Constitutional) |
| Book | Book One — The First Constitutional Epoch |
| Version | 1.0 |

This blessing creates no constitutional, architectural, sprint, or engineering authority.

---

May this institution always value truth above convenience.

May it welcome questions more readily than assumptions.

May every future steward inherit not only its artifacts, but its discipline.

May governance remain thoughtful, architecture remain faithful, engineering remain responsible, and history remain honest.

May future generations find courage to evolve what must evolve, wisdom to preserve what should endure, and humility to know the difference.

May the repository continue to remember with clarity, teach with patience, and guide without demanding certainty.

And whenever a new constitutional epoch begins, may it begin for only one reason:

Because the institution has truly earned the right to ask a new question.

---

End of Blessing
