## Purpose

Unlike the constitutional corpus, the Preservation Series consists of institutional publications.
They create no authority.

Their purpose is to make Project Garuda understandable, teachable, and enduring.

Historians do not study civilizations by reading every government record. They read constitutions,
maps, timelines, letters, atlases, and histories.

Book One records the history. The Preservation Series creates the artifacts that help people
navigate it.
