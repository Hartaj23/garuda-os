# Historical Milestones

## Constitutional Engineering

The institution established the principle that governance should precede architecture and architecture should precede engineering.

This discipline shaped every subsequent stage of the epoch.

## Repository-First Governance

The repository became the institution's permanent memory.

Institutional legitimacy would thereafter depend upon recorded evidence rather than unwritten understanding.

## Foundation Era

The institution progressed through successive constitutional and architectural foundations.

Each foundation clarified a distinct institutional concern.
Each earned its place through explicit governance.

## Architecture 5

Governance itself matured into an institutional discipline.

The institution formally distinguished governance, architecture, and engineering as separate layers of responsibility.

## Governance Cycle 6

The institution examined whether constitutional evolution beyond Runtime remained justified.

The outcome was determined through the established governance process rather than historical expectation.

## GAR-0020

The descriptive external-capability epoch concluded.

Rather than authorizing another constitutional foundation, the institution recognized constitutional completion under the current governing law.

## Constitutional Baseline v1.0

The constitutional corpus reached a stable baseline.

Future constitutional evolution became contingent upon a new governance cycle supported by explicit authority.

## Book One

The institution recorded the history of its own constitutional evolution.

Narrative became part of institutional preservation.

## Preservation Series

The institution began creating navigational literature.

History became easier to understand without altering constitutional authority.

# Reading This Plate

This timeline illustrates institutional evolution rather than document chronology.

Several streams of work progressed in parallel.

Constitutional governance, architectural interpretation, engineering implementation, and institutional literature together formed a single coherent epoch.

The sequence therefore reflects conceptual progression rather than exact calendar dates.

---

# Key Insight

The First Constitutional Epoch was not defined by the number of artifacts it produced.

It was defined by the gradual transformation of Project Garuda from a software initiative into a governed institution capable of explaining its own authority, preserving its own history, and preparing responsibly for future generations.

The epoch concludes not with expansion, but with stewardship.

Institutional HOLD is therefore not the end of the journey.
It is the first enduring expression of institutional maturity.
