# Constitutional Progression

## GAR-0017

The institution recognized Interface as a distinct constitutional concern.

The external boundary became part of the constitutional corpus.

## GAR-0018

The institution recognized Integration as a distinct constitutional concern.

Relationships among external capabilities received constitutional legitimacy.

## GAR-0019

The institution established Runtime as a distinct descriptive constitutional foundation.

Runtime completed the descriptive understanding of external capability state.

## GAR-0020

Rather than authorizing another foundation, GAR-0020 concluded that no further descriptive external-capability foundation was constitutionally admissible under the governing law.

The result was an epoch pause rather than continued expansion.

---

# Constitutional Pattern


The constitutional corpus did not expand indefinitely.
It matured toward a complete descriptive baseline.

# The Turning Point

GAR-0020 differs from every previous constitutional extension.

GAR-0017, GAR-0018, and GAR-0019 each admitted a new constitutional foundation.
GAR-0020 admitted none.

Instead, it demonstrated that disciplined constitutional governance sometimes concludes that no additional foundation is justified.

This decision established the constitutional legitimacy of the descriptive epoch pause.

# Reading the Timeline

The constitutional timeline should not be interpreted as a roadmap for continuous expansion.

It represents the historical evolution of the First Constitutional Epoch.

Future constitutional epochs, if they occur, begin through new governance cycles supported by explicit authority and constitutional necessity.

The timeline records what occurred.
It does not predict what must come next.

---

# Key Insight

The constitutional history of Project Garuda is not a story of perpetual growth.

It is the story of an institution that expanded only when constitutional evidence justified expansion, and that accepted completion when the evidence demonstrated that the descriptive external-capability layer had reached its natural conclusion.

The Constitutional Timeline therefore records more than institutional milestones.
It records the disciplined evolution of constitutional legitimacy.
