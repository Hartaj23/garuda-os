# The Institutional Layers

Project Garuda is organized into six enduring institutional domains.

### Constitutional Corpus

Defines the governing constitutional principles of the institution.

Examples:

- GAR-0017
- GAR-0018
- GAR-0019
- GAR-0020

### Architectural Corpus

Interprets constitutional intent into coherent architectural foundations.

Examples:

- ADR-0011
- ADR-0012
- ADR-0013

### Governance Corpus

Records how constitutional authority is exercised.

Includes:

- Governance Framework
- Governance Cycles
- Founder Decisions
- Gate Authorizations
- Ratifications
- Institutional HOLD

### Repository

The repository serves as the institution's permanent memory.

It preserves:

- constitutional history,
- architectural history,
- governance history,
- engineering history,
- institutional literature.

The repository is the canonical institutional record.

### Institutional Literature

Documents that explain the institution without creating authority.

Examples include:

- Book One — *The First Constitutional Epoch*
- Constitutional Baseline v1.0
- Preservation Series

### Stewardship

The institution is maintained through stewardship rather than continuous expansion.

Preservation, explanation, and disciplined governance ensure continuity across generations.

Project Garuda is more than a software repository.

history, and institutional literature together preserve both what was built and the reasoning that
justified its creation.
