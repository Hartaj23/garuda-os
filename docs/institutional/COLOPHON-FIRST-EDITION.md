# Colophon

## The First Edition

| Field | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Document Type | Institutional Literature |
| Authority | Informational (Non-Constitutional) |
| Edition | First Edition |
| Version | 1.0 |

This colophon creates no constitutional, architectural, sprint, or engineering authority.

---

This First Edition of the **Project Garuda Preservation Series** was prepared during the Preservation Epoch following the completion of the First Constitutional Epoch.

It was created after the institution had established:

- a ratified Constitutional Baseline,
- a complete Architectural Baseline,
- a recorded Governance history,
- an implemented Engineering foundation,
- and a mature practice of Institutional Stewardship.

The purpose of this edition is preservation.

It introduces no constitutional authority.

It amends no governance record.

It modifies no architectural interpretation.

It authorizes no engineering activity.

Its responsibility is to present the institution faithfully.

The Preservation Series was prepared according to the principles that guided Project Garuda throughout its first constitutional generation:

- evidence before assumption,
- authority before action,
- repository before recollection,
- stewardship before expansion.

Every effort has been made to preserve the institution's language, chronology, and reasoning without altering the authority of the canonical repository.

Where differences ever arise between this edition and the repository, the repository remains the authoritative institutional record.

This edition is therefore a companion to the repository rather than a replacement for it.

It exists so that future stewards may approach the institution with understanding before responsibility.

No institution is preserved by documents alone.

It is preserved by people who choose, generation after generation, to understand before they alter, to question before they conclude, and to leave behind a clearer inheritance than the one they received.

If this edition contributes, even in a small measure, to that continuing responsibility, then it has fulfilled its purpose.

The First Edition is complete.

The institution continues.

Its future now belongs to those who will steward it with the same discipline through which it was first preserved.

---

End of Colophon — The First Edition
