# Founding Generation Declaration

| Field | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Document Type | Institutional Literature |
| Authority | Informational (Non-Constitutional) |
| Version | 1.0 |

This declaration creates no constitutional, architectural, sprint, or engineering authority.

---

The Founding Generation of Project Garuda respectfully records that the work entrusted to the First Constitutional Epoch has been completed.

During this epoch, the institution established its constitutional foundations, architectural interpretations, governance discipline, engineering practices, preservation framework, and public philosophy.

These works were created not as an end in themselves, but as an inheritance for those who will one day assume responsibility for the institution.

The Founding Generation recognizes that no institution is ever finished.

Only epochs are.

Future generations will encounter questions that could not have been anticipated here.

They will possess knowledge unavailable to us.

They will work within technologies we cannot yet imagine.

They should not be bound by our conclusions.

They should, however, understand the discipline through which those conclusions were reached before deciding how the institution should evolve.

To every future steward:

Receive this inheritance with gratitude.

Examine it with honesty.

Question it with evidence.

Extend it with legitimacy.

Preserve it with care.

And when your own generation's work is complete, leave behind an institution that is clearer, wiser, and more understandable than the one you received.

If you do that, then the purpose of the First Constitutional Epoch will have been fulfilled.

The Founding Generation now places this institution into the continuing care of those who will inherit it.

May they steward it wisely.

---

End of Founding Generation Declaration
