# Resolution of the Founding Generation

| Field | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Document Type | Institutional Literature |
| Authority | Informational (Non-Constitutional) |
| Version | 1.0 |

This resolution creates no constitutional, architectural, sprint, or engineering authority.

---

The Founding Generation of Project Garuda acknowledges that the First Constitutional Epoch has reached its intended conclusion.

The institution now possesses:

- a constitutional foundation,
- an architectural foundation,
- a governance history,
- an engineering baseline,
- a preservation library,
- and a public philosophy.

These works together establish a coherent institutional inheritance.

The responsibility of the Founding Generation is therefore no longer continual creation.

It is faithful transition.

Accordingly, the Founding Generation adopts the following commitments.

**First.**

The constitutional corpus shall remain the authoritative foundation of the institution until legitimately evolved through future governance.

**Second.**

The repository shall continue to serve as the institution's canonical source of truth.

**Third.**

The Preservation Series shall remain available as the educational companion through which future stewards may understand the institution before seeking to evolve it.

**Fourth.**

Future constitutional evolution shall arise from demonstrated institutional need rather than from the desire for continual expansion.

**Fifth.**

The institution shall welcome thoughtful examination, responsible criticism, and sincere collaboration from those who engage its ideas with intellectual honesty.

The Founding Generation therefore considers its principal responsibility fulfilled.

From this point onward, the institution enters a new phase.

Its success will be measured not by how many additional documents are produced, but by how faithfully its principles are practiced.

May every future generation remember that enduring institutions are not defined by the volume of their work.

They are defined by the quality of their judgment.

With this resolution, the Founding Generation entrusts Project Garuda to its future.

The First Constitutional Epoch is complete.

The institution continues.

---

End of Resolution of the Founding Generation
