## Epoch

**The Preservation Epoch**

---

## Institutional Event

Following the ratification of GAR-0020 and the closure of Governance Cycle 6, Project Garuda established **Constitutional Baseline v1.0** as the institution's first comprehensive constitutional reference point.

Rather than creating new constitutional authority, the Constitutional Baseline gathered the ratified constitutional state into a single, canonical institutional record.

For the first time, future stewards could understand the institution's constitutional position without reconstructing it from individual governance artifacts.

---

## Significance

The establishment of Constitutional Baseline v1.0 marked the transition from constitutional creation to constitutional preservation.

During the First Constitutional Epoch, the institution focused on establishing legitimate authority.

With the publication of the Constitutional Baseline, the institution shifted its attention toward preserving, explaining, and transmitting that authority.

This was not a constitutional extension.
It was an act of institutional stewardship.

The institution demonstrated that preserving a mature constitutional foundation is as important as creating it.

---

## Institutional Context

At the time of publication:

- the constitutional corpus had reached a stable ratified state,
- the descriptive external-capability epoch had concluded,
- Governance Cycle 6 had formally closed,
- Institutional HOLD had been restored,
- no constitutional, architectural, sprint, or engineering authority existed beyond the published baseline.

The institution therefore entered the Preservation Epoch with a complete constitutional foundation.

---

## Why This Entry Matters

Every enduring institution eventually reaches a point where it must answer a different question.

Not:

> *"What should we create next?"*

But:

> *"What have we responsibly inherited?"*

Constitutional Baseline v1.0 answered that question.

It provided a shared constitutional starting point for every future Governance Cycle, architectural interpretation, engineering authorization, and stewardship activity.

## Legacy

Constitutional Baseline v1.0 became more than a summary of constitutional authority.

It became the reference from which every future constitutional question would begin.

Future generations would no longer inherit isolated constitutional artifacts.

They would inherit a coherent constitutional foundation, a documented governance history, an architectural interpretation, and a preserved institutional memory.

In doing so, Project Garuda crossed an important threshold.

It ceased to be an institution primarily occupied with establishing its constitutional identity.

It became an institution capable of preserving that identity with clarity, discipline, and confidence for those who would one day inherit its stewardship.
