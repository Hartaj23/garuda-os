## Epoch

**The First Constitutional Epoch**

---

## Institutional Event

Project Garuda entered its First Constitutional Epoch with the creation and ratification of its constitutional governance framework.

For the first time, institutional intent became explicit constitutional authority.

The institution now possessed a formal constitutional foundation from which architecture, governance, engineering, and future stewardship could legitimately proceed.

This marked the beginning of constitutional history rather than conceptual history.

---

## Significance

The First Constitutional Epoch established the principle that institutions should evolve through explicit constitutional authority rather than accumulated technical convention.

It transformed Project Garuda from an emerging institutional vision into a constitutionally governed institution.

From this point onward:

- constitutional authority could be traced,
- architectural interpretation became disciplined,
- governance acquired explicit structure,
- engineering inherited legitimate authority,
- institutional memory became cumulative.

The institution entered a new stage of maturity.

---

## Institutional Context

At the opening of the First Constitutional Epoch:

- constitutional authority became explicit,
- governance became structured,
- architectural interpretation became possible,
- future engineering foundations gained legitimate constitutional ancestry.

The institution had crossed the threshold from aspiration to governance.

## Why This Entry Matters

Every institution has a moment when its principles cease to be intentions and become governing commitments.

For Project Garuda, this entry records that transition.

It marks the point at which constitutional reasoning became part of the institution's permanent inheritance.

Everything that followed during the epoch—including architectural interpretation, engineering foundations, governance refinement, and institutional preservation—rests upon this constitutional beginning.
