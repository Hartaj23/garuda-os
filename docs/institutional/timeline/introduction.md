# Why This Timeline Exists

This introduction creates no constitutional, architectural, sprint, or engineering authority.

---

Every institution possesses a history.

Only a few preserve that history in a form that future generations can navigate quickly, confidently, and accurately.

Project Garuda has chosen to preserve not only its constitutional artifacts, architectural decisions, governance records, engineering milestones, and institutional literature, but also the chronological relationships among them.

This Timeline exists to make those relationships visible.

It is not a constitutional artifact.
It creates no authority.
It authorizes no governance.
It establishes no architecture.
It permits no engineering.

Its purpose is preservation.

Where Book One tells the story of the First Constitutional Epoch, the Institutional Timeline records its sequence.

Where the Institutional Atlas explains institutional structure, the Timeline explains institutional progression.

Where the Companion defines institutional language, the Timeline shows when that language emerged through governance and practice.

Every entry within this volume records four elements.

- **Date or Period** — when the milestone occurred.
- **Institutional Event** — what happened.
- **Significance** — why the milestone mattered.
- **Repository References** — where the canonical record may be found.

Together, these entries create a chronological map of institutional evolution.

The Timeline is intended to be read in either of two ways.

A new steward may read it from beginning to end to understand how Project Garuda evolved over time.

An experienced steward may consult individual entries to locate a specific constitutional, architectural, governance, engineering, or preservation milestone within the broader institutional history.

The Timeline does not replace the repository.
It provides orientation within it.

Its purpose is simple.

To ensure that every future steward can answer not only **what** the institution became and **why** it became that way, but also **when** each important transition occurred.

Time gives meaning to sequence.
Sequence gives meaning to evolution.

By preserving both, Project Garuda ensures that its history remains as understandable as its constitutions, as navigable as its architecture, and as enduring as its institutional memory.

That is the purpose of this Timeline.
